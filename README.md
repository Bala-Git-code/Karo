# Karo Pitch – Demo Landing Page

A modern startup-style landing page designed for **Karo Pitch**, an initiative by **KaroStartup** that enables founders across India—especially from Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities—to pitch their startups directly to investors.

This project was created as part of an **internship assignment** to design a demo homepage that clearly communicates the platform’s vision and encourages founders to apply.

## 🎯 Project Objective

The goal of this project is to design a **clean, modern, and investor-focused landing page** that communicates:

- The purpose of Karo Pitch
- How founders can apply
- The categories of startups eligible
- The connection between founders and investors
- The credibility of KaroStartup as the parent platform

The design follows modern **startup ecosystem UI patterns** with a strong focus on readability, storytelling, and user experience.

---

## ✨ Features

### Hero Section
A clear introduction to Karo Pitch with a strong call-to-action encouraging founders to apply.

### About Karo Pitch
Explains the mission of the platform and how it bridges the gap between founders and investors.

### How It Works
A simple step-by-step explanation of the pitching process.

1. Apply with your startup pitch
2. Get shortlisted by KaroStartup
3. Pitch to investors
4. Raise funding and scale

### Who Can Apply
Displays categories of startups eligible to participate such as:

- D2C Brands
- Consumer Tech
- MSMEs
- SaaS Startups
- Manufacturing
- Bharat-focused Startups

### Featured Startups
Example startup cards showcasing potential businesses that could pitch on the platform.

### About KaroStartup
Highlights the credibility of KaroStartup as one of India’s largest startup storytelling platforms.

### Final Call to Action
Encourages founders to **Apply to Pitch** or **Partner with the platform**.

## 🎨 Design Principles

The interface was designed based on three main principles:

### 1. Clarity of Vision
The page structure follows a storytelling flow:

Hero → Platform Mission → How It Works → Eligibility → Investors → Featured Startups → Call to Action

This helps visitors quickly understand the platform.

### 2. Startup Ecosystem Aesthetic
The design uses a **clean SaaS-style interface** inspired by modern startup platforms.

Key visual elements include:

- Warm neutral background tones
- Orange accent colors representing energy and growth
- Card-based UI for easy scanning
- Subtle elevation and hover interactions

### 3. User Experience
The page prioritizes:

- clear typography
- balanced spacing
- mobile-friendly layouts
- easy navigation
